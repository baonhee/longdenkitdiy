The Story Behind Our Lantern Kit

For generations, Vietnamese artisans have handcrafted lanterns from bamboo and colorful paper, creating glowing symbols of childhood memories and family unity during Mid-Autumn Festival.

In many villages, children gather around parents and grandparents to cut paper, bend bamboo frames, and light lanterns together. These moments represent connection, creativity, and cultural identity.

Vietnam DIY Lantern Kit was created to bring this authentic tradition to modern families in Vietnam and overseas Vietnamese communities in the United States. It allows parents and children to build lanterns together while learning Vietnamese cultural stories.

Authentic Handmade Lantern Craft

Our kit includes bamboo frames, Vietnamese paper, LED lights, and cultural storytelling cards inspired by real Vietnamese lantern-making villages.
